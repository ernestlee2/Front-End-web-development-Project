About us

Basque Kitchen by Aitor was first established in October 2018 as Singapore’s first Basque-centric restaurant celebrating the country’s indigenous culture and produce. The restaurant was first located on 97 Amoy Street and is a collaboration between Unlisted Collection and Head Chef Aitor Jeronimo Orive – formerly of the Michelin-starred kitchens of The Fat Duck and Mugaritz. The restaurant is now relocated to the Fullerton Waterboat house.

The restaurant showcases contemporary Basque cuisine reinterpreted by Chef-Patron Aitor Jeronimo Orive. Comprising a 28-seater dining room and a private room boasting fine views across the waterfront. Basque Kitchen by Aitor is open for both lunch and dinner, offering an exotic tasting menu for each of the sessions.
