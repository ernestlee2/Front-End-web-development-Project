Our Chefs

ANNE-SOPHIE-PIC

Anne-Sophie Pic family’s illustrious culinary heritage spans more than a century since her great grandmother established L'Auberge du Pin in 1889. The three-Michelin-starred, French chef Anne- Sophie Pic hails from a long lineof Michelin-starred-chefs.

DI MARZIO

Di Marzio brings with him over a decade of culinary experience in Michelin-starred restaurants across theglobe.His passion for culinary expression began as a child where he was inspired by his grandmother’s expression of love through cooking

JUSTIN WEE

Justin Wee is armed with a qualification from the prestigious Court of Master Sommeliers, and a bachelor’s degree in culinary arts with restaurant management from Paul Bocuse Institute.Wee curates a wine list that leans towards French regions

Speciality

Pigeon from Bresse

A juicy, tender and delicate piece of meat covered
by golden brown and crispy skin makes it a definite
once in a lifetime experience

Saga Wagyu Beef

Have a taste of the saga wagyu beef imported directly
from Japan. The tender, smoky and flavorful beef
,cooked to perfection paired with smoked beetroot
rose and lovage makes this the perfect savory
dish and a definite must try while you're there

Organic Welsh Lamb

While you're there, try out the organic and succulent
welsh lamb marinated in melilot and Chartreuse which
brings the prefect balance between sweet and savory

Milk Punch

This sweet,citrusy and creamy drink that can be served
either hot or cold will definitly sweep you off your feet
upon the first sip
