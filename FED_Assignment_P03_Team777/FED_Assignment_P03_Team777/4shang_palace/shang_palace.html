ABOUT

Restaurant Background

Established since 1971, Shang Palace restaurant memorializes the elegance, luxuriance, and dynamism of Cantonese cuisine. While its flavours are redolent of tradition, they are also masterfully crafted with modernism in mind. This harmony of the past and present is preserved by a team of brilliant chefs. On the floor, asian grace and hospitality are nurtured as families feast from one generation to another.

At Shang Palace, legacies are upheld, and new ones are born. A testament to Shang Palace’s commitment to its pursuit, the restaurant has attained the coveted 1 MICHELIN star in the 2021 & 2022 MICHELIN Guide Singapore, and it continues to delight the palates of its patrons.

Meet the Chef

Executive chinese chef Daniel Cheung now helms Shang Palace Singapore, bringing with him decades in world-class Cantonese cooking. The award-winning chef’s experience includes decade-long tenures in some of the most prestigious and revered institutions in his native country Hong Kong. Some of his accolades include a gold award for Creative Appetisers at 2013 HOFEX Modern Chinese Cuisine Challenge; and the Distinction Gold Award for Appetisers at the 10th Asia Culinary Exchange 2016. Combining a traditional foundation with contemporary creativity, Chef Daniel infuses time-honoured classics with modernity and meticulous presentation.
